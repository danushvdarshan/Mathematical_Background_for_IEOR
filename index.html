📐 IIT Bombay IEOR Core Syllabus Framework

Click any domain header below to inspect the reference benchmark math structures required at IITB for profound analytical depth.

  • Systems of linear equations & Gaussian elimination
  • Fields, Vector Spaces, and Subspaces
  • Linear independence, Basis, and Dimension
  • Fundamental matrix subspaces (Column, Row, and Null spaces)
  • Rank-nullity theorem & Duality
  • Linear transformations
  • Eigenvalues, Eigenvectors, and Characteristic polynomials
  • Cayley-Hamilton Theorem
  • Matrix Diagonalization & Canonical forms (Jordan forms)
  • Unimodularity and Total Unimodularity (TUM matrices)
  • Linear Programming foundations (Geometry/Algebra of the Simplex, Extreme points, Basic Feasible Solutions)
  • LP Duality theory, Primal-Dual relationships, and Complementary slackness
  • Sensitivity analysis
  • First and second-order optimality conditions (Necessity vs. Sufficiency)
  • Unconstrained vs. Constrained optimization (Lagrange multipliers & Karush-Kuhn-Tucker/KKT conditions)
  • Gradient Descent, Newton’s Method, and Hessian-based algorithms (Conjugate directions, Quasi-Newton)
  • Convex Optimization (Convex sets, Convex functions, Lagrangian duality)
  • Polyhedral Theory (Faces, face-lattice, facet/vertex complexity)
  • Discrete/Combinatorial Optimization (Integer Programming, Branch-and-Bound, Mixed-Integer Linear Programming, Binary variable constraint modeling)
  • Network Flows (Shortest Path, Maximum Flow, Min-Cost Flow, Max-Flow Min-Cut Theorem)
  • Total Dual Integrality (TDI), Matroids, Polymatroids, and Submodular function minimization
  • Sample spaces, Events, and Axiomatic probability
  • Conditional probability, Independence, and Bayes Theorem
  • Random variables (Discrete & Continuous), Probability Mass Functions (PMF), Probability Density Functions (PDF), and Cumulative Distribution Functions (CDF)
  • Joint, Marginal, and Conditional distributions
  • Mathematical Expectation, Variance, Covariance, Correlation, Moments, and Moment Generating Functions (MGF)
  • Standard Distributions (Bernoulli, Binomial, Poisson, Exponential, Uniform, Normal)
  • Limit Theorems (Weak/Strong Laws of Large Numbers, Central Limit Theorem, Functional CLT)
  • Measure-theoretic probability (Sigma-algebras, Probability Measures, Integration, Lp spaces)
  • Sampling statistics & Order statistics
  • Parameter estimation (Point estimation, Maximum Likelihood Estimators/MLE, Interval estimation, Bias, Efficiency, Consistency)
  • Hypothesis testing (Type I/II errors, t-test, Chi-square tests, Likelihood ratio test, Neyman-Pearson lemma)
  • Stochastic processes definition, Sample paths, and Finite-dimensional distributions
  • Memory-less property, Renewal processes, Regenerative processes, and Renewal reward theorem
  • Discrete-Time Markov Chains (Countable state space, Chapman-Kolmogorov equations, Hitting/Stopping times, Strong Markov property, State classification, Recurrence/Transience, Invariant/Stationary distributions, Limiting probabilities, Ergodic theorem)
  • Continuous-Time Markov Chains (Poisson processes, Jump chains, Sojourn times, Birth-death processes, Kolmogorov forward/backward equations, Uniformization, Time-reversed chains)
  • Martingales (Super/Sub-martingales, Doob’s inequalities, Martingale convergence theorems)
  • Random walks, Ladder chains, and Brownian motion (continuous paths, non-differentiability, hitting times)
  • Queueing Theory (M/G/1, GI/M/1, G/G/1 queues, Little’s Law, PASTA, Drift criteria, Fluid models)
  • Multivariable calculus (Gradients, Jacobians, Hessians, Taylor series expansion)
  • Ordinary Differential Equations (ODEs) (First, second, and higher-order linear ODEs, systems of ODEs, Existence and uniqueness theorems)
  • Difference equations (solutions and stability)
  • Integral transforms (Laplace transforms, Z-transforms)
  • Linear Time-Invariant (LTI) systems, Transfer functions, State-space representation, and Linearization of non-linear systems
  • Control theory foundations (Stability, Controllability, Observability)
